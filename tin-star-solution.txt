Tin Star, release 3
By Gianluca Girelli & Garry Francis
For Z-code interpreters

Solution by Garry Francis
----------------------------------------
This is a minimal solution. You'll need to EXAMINE lots of things for this solution to make sense. Some optional (but significant) commands are shown in parentheses. These can be omitted once you know what to do.

The game starts in a desert. You can map this using the conventional method of dropping items in rooms to make them unique. You can acquire a few items from the starting location and you can find some more nearby. However, a thirst daemon starts when you leave your starting location and you die of thirst after 20 moves, so your first priority should be to find water, then map the rest of the desert.

You will need to ride your horse to cross long distances, but your horse won't let you mount until his thirst has been quenched and you can't mount him until you've eaten something to replenish your energy, so these are your next priorities.

You can find a couple of non-player characters along the way, so ask them about things of interest and anything significant that they mention for some important clues.

There is a HINT command, but only use this if you're desperate. You shouldn't need it, as everything you need is clued within the game.
